ATARASHII GAKKO!

Japanese four-piece dance vocal performance group.

Atarashii Gakko! known in Japan as Atarashii Gakkou no Leaders (新しい学校のリーダーズ meaning 'New School Leaders'), is a Japanese girl group formed in 2015.
